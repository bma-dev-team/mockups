AI Draft · April 2026

April 2026 closed at $487,290 in revenue, up 8.2% from March and tracking 14% ahead of the quarterly plan. Wholesale recovered the seasonal softness from Q1, contributing $324,443 against a March base of $287,118. DTC volume held flat at $162,847 even as AOV climbed to $189.42, suggesting customers spent more per order but order count was roughly unchanged.

Three signals worth attention this month. Gross profit margin compressed by 80 basis points to 60.3%, driven by a one-time inventory write-down of $12,400 in the warehouse audit. Cash position improved by $94,200 month-over-month despite the dividend distribution, reflecting strong AR collection and a longer payment cycle on the largest wholesale invoice. Customer repeat-order share continued its slow climb to 41.7% of new orders, the highest level in the trailing twelve months.

Looking forward to May, the planned vendor consolidation should remove roughly $3,200 of monthly subscription overhead, and the inventory write-down headwind is non-recurring. Plan adherence remains strong.
