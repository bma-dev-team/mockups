Changes made · May 17, 2026

What changed, based on your feedback.

Five suggestions from you. Five fixes shipped to the platform.

ForMalindi
FromBMA Dev Team
PlatformBuildMyApp

Your feedback on the BuildMyApp "request" posting process was specific and genuinely useful, so we built against all five of your suggestions.

Each entry below is your concern in your own words, followed by what we changed. Screenshots are included where the change is visual.

01

Credit card required before posting

Shipped
You said
"Why do I have to put my credit card in before I post?"
We shipped
We removed the requirement entirely. Buyers no longer enter credit card information at posting. Card details are collected only at the moment you accept a developer's proposal, when you actually need a payment method on file.
02

No-negotiation pricing and lack of recourse

Shipped
You said
"Six months seems like a long time to wait to repost with a different price. I also don't understand why price can't be negotiated. It feels scary to spend thousands of dollars with no recourse or way to get my money back."
We shipped
We built an in-product FAQ system that explains why BuildMyApp uses haggle-free pricing (it protects both buyers and developers from races to the bottom and from scope creep), and a second FAQ that lays out the buyer protections already built into every engagement: funds are held in escrow, milestone releases are buyer-approved, and you retain full control over fund release until you confirm delivery against the acceptance criteria you wrote.
Haggle-free pricing FAQ
Buyer protections FAQ
03

Hard to find the full specification

Shipped
You said
"It's a little confusing as to where to find that full specification. I ultimately found it on the left hand side, but that does not feel like the obvious place to look."
We shipped
We rebuilt the request-creation UX. The completed specification now lives on the right-hand side where the eye expects it, and we added live user-feedback signaling that updates as the specification builds out, so it's obvious in real time that your input is becoming the spec.
Revised layout, spec on the right
Live spec-building signal
04

Public visibility of postings

Shipped
You said
"The public posting feels really vulnerable and scary to me. Is there a way to obfuscate parts of it? Or to make the project a little bit hidden until a developer clicks in so that it's not as exposed to the public and more exposed to people who might be interested in bidding on it?"
We shipped
We implemented your recommendation directly. Buyers can now mark a posting as Verified Developers Only, which hides the full request from public view and exposes it only to developers BuildMyApp has verified. The toggle lives in the request form itself, so you choose visibility at posting time.
Visibility toggle on the request form
Verified-Developers-Only applied
05

Pricing felt high

Shipped
You said
"The pricing seems really high."
We shipped
You were right. Our original pricing analysis came straight from base LLM training data, which over-weights agency-style rates. We rebuilt the framework to assume AI-supported independent developers, not agency teams, and to factor in the speed gains of AI-leveraged development. The result is a meaningfully lower price band for builds of your shape.

Specific feedback from real buyers is how a marketplace becomes one that buyers actually trust. Thank you for giving us yours, and for the chance to show what we did with it.
